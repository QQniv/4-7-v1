# 4-7 Rebrand: Art Direction + Migration Plan

Selected direction (February 12, 2026): **Stone Tech Premium**.

## Art direction 01: Stone Tech Premium (recommended)
- Mood: premium + engineering confidence.
- Visual language: large typography, deep green/graphite base, soft mint highlights, glassy surfaces.
- Typography pair: `Prata` (headlines) + `Space Grotesk/Manrope` (UI/text).
- Distinctive details: gradient atmosphere, product cards with short specs, project-first storytelling.
- Strong fit from references:
  - BRAER: strong product blocks and catalog visibility.
  - Belgard / Techo-Bloc: "inspiration -> choose product -> request estimate" flow.
  - Modern Stones / The Stone Collection: premium imagery and showroom tone.

## Art direction 02: Gallery Industrial
- Mood: architectural gallery + tactile material lab.
- Visual language: light concrete background, monochrome typography, terracotta/oxide accent, oversized white space.
- Typography pair: `Instrument Serif` (hero) + `Suisse/Neue Haas style` equivalents.
- Distinctive details: modular cards, horizontal project strips, detail zooms for textures.
- Strong fit from references:
  - Modern Stones / The Stone Collection: gallery layout and premium calmness.
  - Techo-Bloc: structured product data and technical transparency.
